Technical university of Kenya have continuously been giving members of the institution right for expression, whereby individuals can post their info on suggestion box based on feeling they face within the environment. Advancement of technology in information science has greatly impacted the institution curriculum particularly in information sharing i.e. introduction of E-suggestion box system.

Reason for advancing to newly automated system is that it will get rid of some of the challenges that the manual system have been facing these include insecurity, cost movement, time consumption etc. E-suggestion box system will convert all tasks that are carried out by manual system installed in several location within the school compound.
